Frome Valley Digital Trails

The concept of Frome Valley Digital Trails is to make walking trails that
highlight some of the areas amazing natural assets, wonderful trees,
interesting spots for bugs and butterflies and ancient woodlands.

Explore our curated walking routes along the Frome Valley region

For those who want to learn more about their surrounding habitats,
QR codes found on each route can be scanned to provide a glimpse into nature

  1. 1. Choose the trail that you like
  2. 2. Start the trail by clicking the button on its page
  3. 3. Scan the QR codes along the way to learn more about the area
  4. 4. Answer a quiz question at the end of the info page to earn a sticker
  5. 5. Enjoy your walk!

If you do not want to scan the QR codes, you can still access the information about each
route point by opening the drop down menu "Route Points" on the trail's page.

You can also read the written directions by opening them with the "Route Directions" button.
